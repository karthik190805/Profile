Blind Stick

  • Components:
    • Arduino UNO: The brain of the system.
    • Ultrasonic Sensor: Detects obstacles.
    • Buzzer: Makes a sound when an obstacle is nearby.
    • LED: Lights up as a visual alert.
    • Battery: Powers the stick.
  • Functionality:
    • The stick scans the area using the ultrasonic sensor.
    • If it detects an obstacle, the buzzer beeps, and the LED lights up.
    • The user can hear the beep and avoid collisions
